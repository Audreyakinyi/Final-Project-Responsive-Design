Your Mental Health Journey Starts Here

Professional support for a happier, healthier you

How do I book a therapy session?

You can easily book a session through our website by selecting your preferred therapist and available time slot. Complete the booking by providing your details and making a secure payment.

Are online therapy sessions available?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online therapy sessions. Our secure video platform allows you to connect with your therapist from the comfort of your home.

What's the difference between therapy and coaching?

Therapy focuses on treating mental health conditions and emotional difficulties, while coaching is more goal-oriented and focuses on personal development and achieving specific life goals.

Is my personal information kept confidential?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict confidentiality protocols in compliance with healthcare privacy laws. Your information is securely stored and never shared without your explicit consent, except where required by law.

Do you accept insurance?

We work with several major insurance providers. Please contact our support team with your insurance details, and we'll verify your coverage. We also offer affordable self-pay options and sliding scale fees based on income.

How long are therapy sessions?

Standard therapy sessions are 50 minutes long. Initial consultations may take up to 90 minutes to allow for a comprehensive assessment. We also offer extended sessions (80 minutes) upon request for couples or family therapy.
