Your Style Journey Starts Here

Professional support for a more stylish you

How do I book a style consultation?

You can easily book a consultation through our website by selecting your preferred stylist and available time slot. Complete the booking by providing your details and making a secure payment.

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual style consultations. Our secure video platform allows you to connect with your stylist from the comfort of your home.

What's the difference between personal shopping and styling?

Personal shopping focuses on helping you find specific items, while styling is about creating complete looks that work for your body type, lifestyle, and personal style.

Is my personal style information kept private?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict privacy protocols. Your information is securely stored and never shared without your explicit consent.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards in various denominations. They make perfect gifts for fashion-forward friends and family members.

How long does a styling session last?

Standard styling sessions are 60 minutes long. Initial consultations may take up to 90 minutes to allow for a comprehensive style assessment. We also offer extended sessions for wardrobe overhauls or special events.
